Last Updated: November 17, 2025

CUSTOMER CONSENT: By providing your mobile phone number and placing a pickup order at Z SMOKE SHOP, you expressly consent to receive automated SMS text messages regarding your order status, including order confirmations, ready notifications, and product replacement suggestions.

1. Program Description

Z SMOKE SHOP operates an SMS notification program to provide customers with real-time updates about their pickup orders. This service enhances the customer experience by keeping you informed throughout the order fulfillment process.

2. What Messages You Will Receive

  • Order Confirmation: Immediate confirmation when your order is placed
  • Order Status Updates: Notifications when your order is confirmed and being prepared
  • Ready for Pickup: Alert when your order is ready to be picked up (with 1-hour pickup window)
  • Product Replacements: If an item is unavailable, we may suggest a replacement via SMS
  • Order Reminders: Courtesy reminders about your pending pickup

3. Message Frequency

You may receive up to 5-8 messages per order. Message frequency depends on your order status and any product availability issues. Messages will only be sent during store operating hours (typically 9 AM - 9 PM CST).

4. How to Opt-In

By providing your mobile phone number during the checkout process and clicking "Place Order," you are opting in to receive SMS notifications for that specific order. Each order requires a fresh opt-in.

OPT-IN METHOD: Checkbox on checkout page stating: "I consent to receive SMS notifications about my order status at the phone number provided."

5. How to Opt-Out

You can opt-out at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any SMS message
  • Replying UNSUBSCRIBE to any SMS message
  • Contacting our stores directly (contact information below)

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message, then no further messages will be sent unless you opt-in again for a future order.
